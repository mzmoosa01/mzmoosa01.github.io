A little bit about me....

I grew up in Pretoria, South Africa and studied Informatics at the University of Pretoria. My degree focused more on analyses and design then actual programming, I taught myself web programming in order to be competitive in my final year project. My team went on to win multiple awards for the project and I recieved an award for best student over all three years. Over the last year of being a professional developer I've learned to really appreciate clean, easy to understand code, as well developed a serious distrust towards legacy code bases. I've recently gotten interested in the work of, and work inspired by, Robert C. Martin (Uncle Bob) and like to consider myslef a software craftsmen rather then a software developer.
